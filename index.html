About Us

Elm Capital Partners is a private equity investment firm focused on acquiring and operating businesses with revenue ranging from $5M - $150M with a goal of growing such businesses through long range planning, application of relevant industry best practices, investments in growth initiatives, and careful stewardship of financial performance. We are not limited to any particular industry nor geographic region; instead, our target companies are primarily ones we believe to have a potential for growth and include companies that may be undergoing some element of transition.

The fine balance between challenging the status quo, regardless of a company’s stage, and keeping a clear focus on proven revenue drivers, is a core value that we employ when operating and strategically positioning a business. Through this lens, Elm Capital Partners seeks to drive continuous improvement to generate long-term growth and maximize value.

Another equally important core value is our focus on value-creating initiatives and operational improvements. Consequently, the execution of such strategy and focus is on lock-step collaboration with existing leadership and will require creativity, unique solutions, strong partnership, mutual respect and accountability, and a clear line of honest and candid communication among the management team.

All in all, Elm Capital Partners aims to work in partnership with successful management teams to grow and responsibly develop businesses with a long-term view.

General Partner Bio

Lloyd Umali is a professional with extensive commercial experience, most recently holding various leadership roles at The Walt Disney Company where he spent over 15 years in a wide range of divisions - film theatrical and television distribution, production, participations/residuals, and talent audit, streaming/direct-to-consumer, and linear programming and network ad sales as well as consumer products (vertical and licensing of merchandise and books) and mobile/console games. His experience has been both in the domestic and international space, and includes deal analysis and valuation of various programs, sales planning and analysis, and management of a variety of projects with a goal of streamlining processes and alignment of business units.

Prior to the Walt Disney Company, Lloyd started his professional career at Ernst & Young in the Los Angeles office in 2006 focusing primarily on private equity clients. He always looks for ways to improve processes by challenging the status quo. He also exhibits strong people management and analytical skills.

Lloyd obtained a bachelor’s degree in business administration from California State University, Los Angeles in 2005. While working full-time during the day and raising a family, Lloyd completed his coursework in the evenings and graduated with the highest honors – summa cum laude.
